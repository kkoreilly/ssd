How to reduce your food waste:

  1. Clean out your fridge -- One of the easiest ways to waste food is to not know it exists, hidden in the back of your fridge.
  2. Buy only food that you know you will have -- Make sure to only get things that you are using for a recipe soon or things that you are 100% certain you will eat before they expire.
  3. Keep track of everything in your fridge -- Make sure nothing gets forgotten in the back of your fridge.
  4. Don't make too much food -- Only make how much food you will eat, otherwise things will go to waste.
  5. Don't put too much food on your plate -- You can always get more food, and any extra food on your plate will go to waste.

By following these steps you will reduce your food waste, which will make a positive impact on the world and also help you save money.
